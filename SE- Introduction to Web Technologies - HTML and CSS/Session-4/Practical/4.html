Inception

Genre: Science Fiction | Action

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Review

Inception is a visually stunning science-fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film combines breathtaking action, emotional storytelling, and a complex dream-within-a-dream concept that keeps viewers engaged from beginning to end.

With outstanding performances, excellent visual effects, and an unforgettable soundtrack, it is widely regarded as one of the greatest modern science-fiction films.
